WELLNESS CENTRE

However, you like to relax - poolside, with a massage, manicure or hair styling session, or by sweating it out at the gym - Ole-Sereni has state-of-the-art amenities to make your stay as rejuvenating as possible. Our health club has everything you need to stay in shape and our spa and salon will have you looking your best, while our pool is one of the hotel's best spots for lounging.

HEALTH CLUB

Health Club. Ole-Sereni's health club is open from 6 a.m. to 12 p.m. from Monday to Friday only and gives guests full access to a range of cardio and strength training machines and free weights, all under the supervision of expert trainers who are happy to provide private sessions.

SALON AND SPA

With our advanced treatment rooms, imported products and highly professional technicians, Ole-Sereni's spa and salon offer some of Nairobi's most sophisticated services. From clarifying facials to deeply relaxing massages, our spa menu offers a full range of treatments, including romantic couple's packages.

SWIMMING POOL

Ole Sereni boasts as the ONLY City Hotel in the World by a National Game Park. It has super luxurious roof pool Nairobi. The atmosphere is relaxed and you get to be in harmony with nature here with the Nairobi National Park view and a drink in hand. Here you can take some really cool Instagram-worthy photos at the edge of the pool with Nairobi National Park view as a backdrop. It is definitely one of the best Nairobi rooftop pool you can find.
